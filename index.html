About Me

During my university education in Geomatics Engineering, I had the privilege of acquiring extensive and invaluable academic knowledge pertaining to data collection, organization, and visualization. As I ventured into my professional life, I found myself immersed in diverse projects involving both tabular and unstructured data. One of my key responsibilities was designing ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes for various databases, ensuring seamless data integration and management.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work with a wide array of data visualization tools, skillfully crafting dynamic dashboards that presented critically important insights. Leveraging my strong analytical and problem-solving abilities, I have consistently contributed to identifying and resolving complex challenges. Moreover, my proficiency in understanding and enhancing cross-departmental processes has been invaluable when collaborating with interdisciplinary teams.

As I look towards the future, I am eager to take my career to new heights by specializing in cloud computing services and AI/ML for Data Engineering. I am deeply passionate about the limitless possibilities that these cutting-edge technologies offer in revolutionizing the way data is harnessed, processed, and transformed into valuable business intelligence.

I am excited to embark on this journey of growth and development, continuously expanding my skill set and expertise in Data Engineering.
